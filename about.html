ABOUT ME

My name is Shane, and I am a web developer and graphic designer living in London Ontario, Canada. I am still enrolled in IDP at Fanshawe College aiming to get my degree in a couple months! I love creating anything digitally such as coding, to graphics, to 3D rendering. I am very fluent in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript also with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Cinema 4D. I’m extremely passionate about web development and design in all it’s forms and helping small businesses and artisans build and improve their online presence.
